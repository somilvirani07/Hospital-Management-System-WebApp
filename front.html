The symbol of St. Mary Hospital represents health care and compassion. The crescent is an ancient icon which, when used in red, has become the equivalent of the International Red Cross. The three-crescent design in its simple, poetic form creates an internal space symbolising how institutions and programmes using the symbol surround, attend and care for those in need. With Imperial Private Healthcare, you’ll experience the best of both worlds – internationally renowned consultants providing care in modern, private facilities, all within some of London’s most trusted NHS hospitals. From diagnosis until discharge, you and your family will experience exceptional levels of discretion and care.

“Imperial Private Healthcare delivers word class medical expertise against a background of excellent NHS care and the groundbreaking research of Imperial College London”

About

Constantly expanding, the hospital has been adding new specialities and facilities to cater to its wide range of patients across different cities and countries.
